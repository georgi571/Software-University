Chillhop Essentials - Winter 2023

We introduce to you the first Chillhop Essentials of 2023 with our Spring release: a 25 song compilation available on purple double vinyl. The seasonal release comes complete with an immersive playground for our Racoon to roam. With all art handled by illustrator/animator Rafael Aruajo, the Essentials 2020 universe shows that anything is possible. Follow along and help us explore.
